The Details

How GreenHeart Works

Transparent, fair, and designed around you.

01
💳
Subscribe Monthly or Yearly
Choose your plan. Payments are processed securely via Stripe. Cancel anytime. Yearly plans save 20%.
02
⛳
Log Your Stableford Scores
Enter up to 5 scores (range 1–45). Each must include a date. Your 5 most recent scores are kept. New entries replace the oldest.
03
❤️
Support Your Charity
Pick any charity from our directory. A minimum of 10% of your subscription goes directly to them. You can give more.
04
🎲
Monthly Draw
Every month, 5 numbers are drawn. Your scores are compared. Match 3, 4, or all 5 numbers to win your tier.
05
🏅
Verify & Collect
Winners submit proof via screenshot of their golf platform scores. Our admin team reviews and approves within 48 hours.
06
🔄
Jackpot Rollover
If no one matches all 5 numbers, the jackpot rolls over to next month — growing the prize for every player.
